18 U.S.C. § 2257 Compliance Statement

Last updated: August 6, 2025

1. Compliance Notice

In compliance with United States federal law, specifically 18 U.S.C. § 2257 and 28 CFR Part 75, all visual depictions of sexually explicit conduct displayed on the Xshiver platform are produced in accordance with age verification and record-keeping requirements.

2. Age Verification

All performers, actors, models, and other persons who appear in any visual depiction of actual or simulated sexually explicit conduct on the Xshiver website were at least 18 years of age or older at the time of the creation of such depictions.

3. Record-Keeping Requirements

Required records pursuant to 18 U.S.C. § 2257 and associated regulations, including documentation verifying the ages of all individuals depicted in sexually explicit materials, are maintained by the custodian of records listed below. These records are organized by producer and are made available for inspection as required by law.

4. Exemption

Content that does not contain actual human beings engaged in sexually explicit conduct, or that was created prior to July 3, 1995, is exempt from the record-keeping requirements referenced above.
